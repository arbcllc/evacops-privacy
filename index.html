1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how ARBC, LLC ("ARBC," "we," "us," or "our") handles information through the EvacOps web console, EvacOps Driver mobile application, and related services. EvacOps is an operational platform for authorized personnel managing evacuation assignments, vehicles, rally and muster points, ramp gates, dispatch alerts, and driver navigation.

An employer, public agency, contractor, or other participating organization may administer user accounts and determine how EvacOps is used during an operation. Those organizations may have additional policies that apply to their personnel.

2. Information we process

Depending on your role and how EvacOps is configured, we may process:

  • Account and profile information: name, email address, phone number, company or organization, role, authentication identifier, and account status.
  • Operational information: assigned vehicle, exit, rally point, muster point, gate information, task status, timestamps, and dispatch activity.
  • Location and movement information: precise latitude and longitude, location accuracy, heading, speed, timestamp, and associated assignment or vehicle context.
  • Notification information: device messaging token, notification permission status, alerts sent, and related delivery information available from the messaging service.
  • Technical and security information: device and application configuration needed for authentication, security, troubleshooting, and service delivery.

3. Location and navigation

The Driver App uses precise location to show a driver's operational position, support dispatch awareness, associate the driver with an assignment, and provide directions between the driver's current location, rally points, and muster points. Location information is linked to the authenticated driver's account and may be visible to authorized EvacOps managers and dispatchers.

The current Driver App is designed to send location updates while the app is active and the user is signed in. It attempts to stop active tracking when the app is no longer active or the user signs out. A last-known location or status may remain in the operational system until it is updated, marked inactive, or removed under the applicable retention process.

Google Maps Platform and Google Navigation services may receive current location, destination, and route-request information to provide maps, routing, traffic-aware directions, and turn-by-turn navigation. Your device operating system also controls location permissions.

4. How information is used

We use information to:

  • Authenticate users and control access to operational information.
  • Create, display, and update assignments, vehicles, gates, and task status.
  • Provide maps, navigation, route context, and live driver visibility.
  • Deliver dispatch alerts and operational notifications.
  • Support incident coordination, safety monitoring, and after-action review.
  • Maintain service security, investigate errors, and prevent misuse.
  • Comply with applicable contractual, legal, and operational requirements.

5. How information is shared

Information may be available to:

  • Authorized operational users: managers, dispatchers, administrators, and other personnel with permission to support an operation.
  • Your participating organization: the employer, agency, contractor, or incident organization responsible for your EvacOps account.
  • Service providers: Google Firebase for authentication, database, and cloud messaging; Google Maps Platform and Navigation SDK for mapping and routing; and Apple services used to distribute the iOS application and deliver supported notifications.
  • Legal and safety recipients: when disclosure is required by law, necessary to protect rights or safety, or needed to respond to a valid government request.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use EvacOps data for third-party behavioral advertising.

Related privacy information: Firebase Privacy, Google Privacy Policy, and Apple Privacy Policy.

6. Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide EvacOps, support active operations, maintain security and audit records, complete after-action review, satisfy participating-organization requirements, and comply with applicable law. Retention periods may vary by information type, customer, incident, or contractual requirement.

When information is no longer required, it may be deleted, anonymized, or retained only as required for legal, security, or operational recordkeeping purposes.

7. Your choices and requests

  • You can control location and notification permissions through your device settings. Disabling them may prevent live tracking, navigation, or dispatch-alert features from working correctly.
  • You can sign out of the Driver App to end your authenticated session.
  • To request access, correction, or deletion of account information, contact your EvacOps administrator or the ARBC contact listed below. Requests may be handled in coordination with the organization responsible for the account.

8. Security

EvacOps uses technical and organizational safeguards intended to protect information, including authenticated access, role-aware operational controls, encrypted network connections, and managed cloud services. No system can guarantee absolute security, and users should protect their credentials and promptly report suspected unauthorized access.

9. Children's privacy

EvacOps is a workforce and emergency operations application. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the service.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as EvacOps features, operational practices, or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of the page identifies the latest published version. Material changes may also be communicated through the app or participating organizations when appropriate.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, access or deletion requests, or concerns about EvacOps, contact your organization's EvacOps administrator or contact ARBC, LLC through its corporate website. Please identify EvacOps in your request.
